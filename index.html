What drives me to create with code?

Building and enhancing software to make people's days better is what I do. Passion for learning and improvement has always been part of me. In university, I studied economics where I learned to think strategically and make decisions to optimize the outcome. My minor in Computer Science started me on the path to being a developer. While working in analytics after university I was struck by how even small improvements in systems and processes universally made radical impacts on user happiness and satisfaction.

Between work and pursuing my own outside interests, I have seen just how much the right technology can help people every day. Now I devote my time to building those products and improving the world a little bit at a time.
